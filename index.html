About Me

From a young age, I’ve had the privilege of watching my father work as a freelance graphic designer. He would take on design briefs for everything from web and UI/UX to product design, and I’d sit nearby, fascinated by the creative process. Back then, I wasn’t a designer yet, but armed with Xara and Adobe Fireworks, I would try my hand at redesigning his briefs, determined to do better. My early attempts weren’t much to write home about, but over time, my skills evolved, and so did my passion. Eventually, I found my own path into UI/UX design. Inspired by my dad and older brother, I discovered a love for creating designs that aren’t just visually stunning but also meaningful and functional. I’m lucky to have been blessed with a creative mind and a knack for turning ideas into beautiful, impactful designs.

Read More

At just 13, I landed my first internship at OrangePeelStudios, a freelance-based design agency. I worked alongside the team for three incredible years, during which I learned the fundamentals of design—principles, colour theory, typography, and much more. I’m immensely grateful to OrangePeelStudios for giving me such a strong foundation and even letting me contribute to their YouTube channel. After those three formative years, I decided it was time to move into a more professional work environment. That’s when I joined OvoWebSolutions, a startup design agency with a more structured and professional atmosphere. During my year there, I gained invaluable experience in navigating a professional setting and refining my skills further. Now, I’m ready to take the next step in my journey. I’m seeking opportunities with larger, established organisations where I can prove my abilities, contribute meaningfully, and continue to grow as a designer. I know I have the skills, the creativity, and the drive to make an impact, and I’m excited to show that.
